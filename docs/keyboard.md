## Bluetooth Pairing

### Interface Switch

* Pn + 1 = BT mode
* Pn + 2 = USB mode

![Keyboard buttons showing Pn + 1 for BT mode or 2 for USB mode]

## Keyboard Pairing

1. Turn the BT switch to "ON".
2. Press Pn + 1 to switch to BT mode, then press Pn + F1 / F2 / F3 for about 3 seconds to pair your device (LED2 flash blue and will turn off after pairing).

![Keyboard buttons showing Pn + F1/F2/F3 combinations for different devices]

Device1
Device2
Device3

Pn + F1    --- Device1
Pn + F2    --- Device2
Pn + F3    --- Device3

3. Turn on PC Bluetooth and other devices settings.
4. Select "Add Bluetooth or other devices".

![Screenshot of Windows Bluetooth settings menu]

5. Add a device and select "Mistel1 / Mistel2 / Mistel3".

![Screenshot of Bluetooth device selection menu showing Mistel devices]

6. Complete.

### Multi-device Switch

* Press Pn + F1 / F2 / F3 to switch to different devices (LED2 flash blue for 2 times)
* Maximum pairing for 3 different devices.

*Note: The battery will be fully charged within 2-3 hours. When you operate the RGB LED under BT mode, Build-in lithium battery will be no power after 30 minutes.
